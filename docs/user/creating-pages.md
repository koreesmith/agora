# Creating a Page

## Who should create a Page?

Pages are designed for:
- **Bands & musicians** — share releases, tour dates, updates
- **Businesses** — promote products and connect with customers
- **Organizations & nonprofits** — share news and campaigns
- **Creators & influencers** — grow an audience

## How to create a Page

1. Go to **Pages** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Create Page** (top-right).
3. Follow the 5-step guided wizard:
   - **Name** — the public name of your Page (required)
   - **Bio** — a short description (optional, can be added later)
   - **Type** — Band, Business, Organization, or Creator
   - **Photos** — upload an avatar and cover photo (optional)
   - **Review** — preview your Page and click **Publish**
4. Your Page is live immediately.

## Posting as your Page

- From the main feed, click the **identity dropdown** above the compose box and select your Page.
- Or go directly to your Page profile and click **New Post**.
- Page posts are always **Public**.

## Managing your Page

Go to your Page profile → **Edit** (or `/pages/your-slug/settings`) to:
- Edit the display name, bio, type, and privacy
- Upload or change the avatar and cover photo
- View analytics (subscriber growth, top posts)
- Invite team members (admins and editors)

## Page team members

Owners can invite other users as:
- **Admin** — can post, edit Page settings, manage team
- **Editor** — can post only

Invited users receive a notification and must accept before gaining access.
