# Groups

Groups are shared spaces where members can post and discuss topics together.

## Joining a group

1. Go to **Groups** in the sidebar.
2. Browse the **Discover** section or search by name.
3. Click **Join Group** (public groups) or **Request to Join** (private groups).
4. For private groups, the group owner or moderators will review your request.

## Creating a group

1. Go to **Groups → Create Group**.
2. Enter a name, optional description, and choose **Public** or **Private**.
3. Click **Create**.
4. Customise your group by adding a cover photo and avatar from the group settings.

## Posting in a group

- Navigate to the group and use the compose box at the top of the group feed.
- Posts inside a group are visible only to members (for private groups) or to everyone (for public groups).

## Invite links

Group owners and moderators can create **invite links** under the group settings. Share the link to let people join directly, even if the group is private.

## Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full control — can delete the group, manage all members and settings |
| **Mod** | Can approve join requests, add/remove members, create invite links |
| **Member** | Can read and post |

## Tagging a group in a post

Type **+group-slug** in any post to tag the group. Group admins will receive a notification.
