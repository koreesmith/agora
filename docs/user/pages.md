# Pages

Pages are public presences for bands, businesses, organizations, and creators. Think of them like a public broadcast channel.

## Finding and following Pages

1. Go to **Pages** in the sidebar.
2. Browse **Featured** pages curated by the instance admins, or search by name.
3. Click **Subscribe** to follow a Page. Its posts will appear in your home feed.

You can also search for pages at **Search → Pages tab**.

## What you see from Pages

- Page posts appear inline in your home feed alongside friend posts.
- Page posts are always **Public**.
- You'll receive a **notification** when a Page you follow publishes a new post.

## Unsubscribing

Click **Subscribed** on the Page profile to unsubscribe. The Page's posts will stop appearing in your feed immediately.
