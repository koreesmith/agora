# Reporting Content

If you see a post, comment, or user that violates the community guidelines, you can report it. Reports are reviewed by the instance moderators.

## Reporting a post or comment

1. Click the **⋯ menu** on the post or comment.
2. Select **Report**.
3. Choose the reason (spam, harassment, misinformation, inappropriate content, etc.).
4. Add any optional details and click **Submit**.

You'll see a confirmation message. The person you reported is **not notified** that you filed a report.

## Reporting a user

1. Go to the person's **profile page**.
2. Click the **⋯ menu** → **Report user**.
3. Choose a reason and submit.

## Reporting a Page

1. Go to the Page's profile.
2. Click the **⋯ menu** → **Report page**.
3. Choose a reason and submit.

## After you report

- Moderators review all reports privately.
- You may not receive individual follow-up on each report, but all reports are investigated.
- If the content is found to violate guidelines it will be removed and the account may be suspended.
