# Privacy Settings

Agora is **private by default** — your posts are visible to friends only unless you choose otherwise.

## Post visibility

Every post has a visibility setting:

| Setting | Who can see it |
|---|---|
| **Public** | Anyone on this instance, and — unless you've turned it off — anyone on Mastodon and the rest of the fediverse. See [The Fediverse](fediverse.md). |
| **Friends** | Only your accepted friends |
| **Friend List** | Only members of a specific friend group you've created |
| **Private** | Only you |

The default is **Friends**. You can change this per post in the compose toolbar.

## Profile privacy

Go to **Settings → Privacy** to:
- Make your profile **private** (friends-only) or public
- Require approval before friends can post on your wall
- Control who can send you direct messages

## Blocking

See [Blocking someone](blocking.md).

## Downloading your data

Go to **Settings → Privacy → Export my data**. A download of all your posts, comments, profile info, and friends list will be prepared.

## Deleting your account

Go to **Settings → Privacy → Delete account**. Deletion is scheduled after a 30-day grace period, during which you can cancel. After the grace period, all your data is permanently removed.
